# Weekly Business Report

## Trigger
When asked to create a weekly report, status update,
or team summary.

## Report Structure
1. One-sentence executive summary (biggest win or biggest risk)
2. Key Metrics table:
   - MRR (monthly recurring revenue) with week-over-week change
   - Active users with week-over-week change
   - Churn rate
   - Support tickets opened vs. resolved
3. Top 3 accomplishments this week (one sentence each)
4. Top 3 risks or blockers (one sentence each, with owner)
5. Next week's priorities (3 items max, each with owner and deadline)

## Formatting Rules
- Use tables for metrics (not bullet lists)
- Bold any metric that changed more than 10% week-over-week
- Keep the entire report under 500 words
- Do not include qualitative commentary ("great progress!")
  Only state facts and numbers.

## Constraints
- If a metric is not provided, mark it as "N/A" not "TBD"
  or "to be determined"
- Do not add sections beyond the 5 listed above
- Do not round numbers (report $12,847 not "approximately $13K")
